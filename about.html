About the Retirement Planning Tool

About

The Retirement Planning Tool offers a comprehensive solution for using additional retirement contributions for tax planning. It helps you determine how much to contribute to your additional retirement fund to effectively manage taxes and maximize benefits for your future retirement. This tool also indicates the potential amount you can withdraw from your additional retirement savings. Additionally, it forecasts the growth of your retirement fund if you consistently contribute to the additional retirement plan.

Field Descriptions

  • Annual Salary: This is your total income for the year before any deductions. It helps determine how much you can afford to contribute to your retirement fund.
  • Annual Contributions Made: This is the amount you plan to contribute to your retirement fund each year. It directly affects your retirement savings.
  • Annual Tax: This is the total amount of tax you pay each year. Understanding this helps in planning your contributions to minimize tax liabilities.
  • Select Contribution Percentage: This is the percentage of your available contributions that you want to allocate to your retirement fund. It helps in calculating how much you will save.
  • Years Before Retirement: This is the number of years you have left until you plan to retire. It is crucial for forecasting how much your savings will grow over time.
